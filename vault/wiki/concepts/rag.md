# RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

Traditional approach: embed documents into vectors, retrieve relevant chunks via similarity search. Scales to millions of documents but requires infrastructure. Compare with [[LLM Wiki]] for simpler alternative at moderate scale.
