# Andrej Karpathy

Former Director of AI at Tesla and founding member of OpenAI. One of the most influential AI researchers and educators.

## Key Contributions

- Created the viral [[LLM Wiki]] concept -- building knowledge bases with LLMs and markdown instead of [[RAG]]
- Published the [LLM Wiki Gist](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f) which went viral on X
- Advocates for simplicity: "just markdown files" over complex vector database infrastructure

## Philosophy

- Knowledge should be compiled once and kept current, not re-derived for every query
- Human's job: curate sources, direct analysis, ask good questions, think
- LLM's job: everything else (bookkeeping, connections, summaries, index maintenance)
