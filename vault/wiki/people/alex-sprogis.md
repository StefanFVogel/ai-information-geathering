# Alex Sprogis

Real name Alexander Sprogis. German Product Engineer and Content Creator with 10+ years in Product Management, 150+ software projects. Co-founder of VisualMakers (No-Code/AI education). Runs Sprogis Media UG. Mission: helping non-technical people build software with AI tools.

YouTube creator (@alex.sprogis, ~8.4K subscribers, German-language) focused on [[Claude Code]] workflows, context engineering, and AI-assisted development. Covers full-stack development with AI (Next.js, Tailwind, Supabase, Vercel) and No-Code/Low-Code tools.
